Welcome to Parklife Café

Parklife Café has been open since September 2021. Situated in Crumin Dublin 12 , it is an ideal spot for locals to pop in for a coffee while out for a walk , or for the family to enjoy a good meal. We are local people of Crumlin with a love of the community and area. We love Crumlin and we love Dublin.

The local area is made up primarily of suburban housing estates, there is often a lot of hustle and bustle between joggers, dog walkers , shoppers buying groceries or people out and bout for their 10,000 steps. Because of this, Parklife Café has become a familiar favourite of locals. We chose the location of the Café deliberately so that it would be a perfect spot for Crumlin residents to meet and catch up, without having to travel too far.

We pride ourselves on being dog friendly. On a typical day in the café you can see jackrussels, pugs, labradors, chihuahas and all sorts of furry characters. We are dog owners and dog lovers, and since so many people come in for a coffee while out walking the dogs, it becomes the perfect place to make friends and introduce your tail wagging companions.

In the coffee dept, we partnered with another Dublin business Cloudpicker Coffee. The beans are roasted weekly to create a unique coffee product for the Irish Market. The beans are sourced ethically to ensure the pickers are treated well and fairly. Cloudpicker Coffee focus on sustainability in all aspects of their business. The attention paid to the ethics of the business made them the perfect place to source our beans. You can taste the quality in every cup.

We bake our patented Parklife sourdough bread everyday. So delicious and meticulously made, we use it for all menu items with sandwiches, toast or bread. We also sell the freshly baked loaves to take away, so feel free to grab a loaf after your coffee so you can enjoy it at home.

The dishes on our menu were designed by committee; between ourselves , professional chefs and members of our family. Our goal was to bring a simple café dining experience to Crumlin, while using quality ingredients to make even a simple sandwich memorable. Our menu lists all available items, allergens , vegetarian and vegan options. We are happy to make amendments to any meal to suit your dining experience.

OUR GOALS : provide a friendly local spot for Crumlin residents to pop in for a coffee or a bite to eat. Make it so it feels like a regular spot for locals to feel at home , with high quality food and coffee.

OUR ETHICS : people from all walks of life are welcome in our café , both as customers and employees. We believe everyone is entitled to respect and kindness, we believe our café should be a welcome space for anyone and everyone. The ethics of business are important, from our sustainably + ethically sourced Dublin coffee , to the way we treat our amazing employees, without whom none of this would be possible.
